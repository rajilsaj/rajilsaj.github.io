## Introduction to Custom Design

We are exploring how to create unique designs for individual blog posts within our Jekyll site. By utilizing custom layouts, we can achieve distinct visual styles for different types of content.

### Key Features of this Design

*   **Prominent Title:** The post title is large and bold, ensuring it catches the reader's eye.
*   **Clear Metadata:** Date, author (if specified), and categories are clearly displayed for easy reference.
*   **Readable Content Area:** The main content uses `prose lg:prose-xl max-w-none` classes (from Tailwind Typography) for enhanced readability.
