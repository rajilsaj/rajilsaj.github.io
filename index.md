# About Me

<img src="/caihanlin.jpg" class="floatpic">

Here is **Hanlin Cai** [(Lance, 蔡汉霖)](#).<br>

I am a first-year PhD student in the [Internet of Everything Group](#) at the University of Cambridge, where I am fortunate to be supervised by [Prof. Ozgur Akan](#), and advised by [Prof. Jon Crowcroft](#) and [Dr. Kai Li](#). My research is supported by the Cambridge Trust and CSC Scholarships. Previously, I had the privilege of conducting summer research with [Prof. Pietro Liò](#) in the [Artificial Intelligence Group](#).

Prior to joining Cambridge, I worked on wireless networks and cybersecurity under the guidance of [Prof. Zhezhuang Xu](#) and [Dr. Meng Yuan](#). Additionally, I was honored to be selected as an outstanding undergraduate scholar at leading AI conferences, including AAAI 2024 and SIGKDD 2024.

I am always open to academic discussions and potential collaborations. Please feel free to reach out to me at **hc663 [at] cam.ac.uk**
